# SAF-K8S-0403-026 - Secret leak prevention gate and enforcement controls

- Domain: D04 - Identity, Access, and Secrets Management
- Knowledge Area: 4.3 - Secrets Management
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: baseline

## Statement

Organizations must enforce preventive secret leak controls such as pre-commit checks, push protection, or CI/CD secret detection gates to block publication of exposed credentials where the workflow supports prevention.

## Objective

Prevent credential leaks from being committed or published by enforcing automated blocking controls at supported workflow stages.

## Rationale

Preventive enforcement is independently auditable from broad leak-detection coverage. This control isolates blocking gates and enforcement controls so teams can prove that secret exposure is actively prevented at commit, push, or pipeline stages rather than merely detected after publication.

## Scope

Applies to developer workflows and CI/CD paths where preventive blocking is supported. Covers pre-commit hooks, push protection, pipeline gates, and similar enforcement controls that block publication of exposed credentials. Broad detection coverage remains governed by SAF-K8S-0403-025, and response remains governed by SAF-K8S-0403-015.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Preventive secret leak enforcement in developer and CI/CD workflows.
- Implementation Surface: Pre-commit scanning, push protection, CI/CD secret detection gates, and workflow-blocking controls for exposed credentials.
- Owner Primary: Security Engineering
- Owner Supporting: Platform Engineering; Application Development
