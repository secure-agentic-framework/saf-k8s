# SAF-K8S-0905-051 - Model promotion approval audit binding to reviewer identity and model version

- Domain: D09 - AI Workload Security: Training, Serving, and Pipelines
- Knowledge Area: 9.5 - AI Supply Chain and Model Lifecycle
- Maturity: Advanced
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Model promotion workflows must record an audit trail that binds the authenticated reviewer identity, approval decision, timestamp, and approved model version so promotion decisions remain attributable and reviewable.

## Objective

Ensure that approved model versions have an auditable promotion record linking the reviewer identity and decision details to the promoted model artifact or version.

## Rationale

Even when reviewer identity is authenticated, promotion decisions remain weakly governed if the resulting audit trail does not preserve who approved what and when. Audit binding supports reviewability, non-repudiation, and post-incident reconstruction of production promotion decisions.

## Scope

Applies to all model promotion workflows requiring human approval on Kubernetes. Covers audit records linking reviewer identity, decision time, approval outcome, and promoted model version. Excludes separation-of-duties enforcement, which remains covered by SAF-K8S-0905-025.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Promotion audit logging and decision record retention.
- Implementation Surface: Promotion audit trail records linking approver identity, timestamp, approval decision, and promoted model version, plus retention and review mechanisms for those approval records.
- Owner Primary: Governance
- Owner Supporting: ML Platform Engineering; Security Engineering
