# SAF-K8S-0702-021 - Namespace quota utilization monitoring and exhaustion alerting for GPU and AI workloads

- Domain: D07 - Storage, Multi-tenancy, and Resource Governance
- Knowledge Area: 7.2 - Namespace Isolation and Multi-Tenancy
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Quota utilization for GPU and AI workload namespaces SHALL be continuously monitored, and alerts SHALL be generated before namespace resource quotas are exhausted so operators can prevent tenant disruption, runaway consumption, or billing anomalies.

## Objective

Detect approaching namespace quota exhaustion for GPU and AI workloads before tenant capacity limits cause service disruption, scheduling failure, or unbounded spend.

## Rationale

Namespace quotas only reduce risk if operators can see when tenants are approaching exhaustion. Utilization monitoring and lead-time alerting prevent silent saturation, enable capacity planning, and surface runaway usage before it impacts shared cluster operations or cloud cost.

## Scope

Applies to AI or accelerator-consuming namespaces where quota utilization, saturation trends, or exhaustion risk must be monitored and alerted on for GPU and related constrained resources.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Quota telemetry, dashboards, and alerting integrated with cluster monitoring and operational review processes.
- Implementation Surface: Quota utilization reports, dashboards, alert thresholds, and operational evidence showing approaching exhaustion is detected and acted on before namespace quotas are exceeded.
- Owner Primary: Platform Engineering
- Owner Supporting: ML Engineering; FinOps
