# SAF-K8S-0603-010 - SBOM attestation binding to image digests

- Domain: D06 - Supply Chain, Images, and Admission Control
- Knowledge Area: 6.3 - Attestation, Provenance, and Cryptographic Assurance
- Maturity: Advanced
- Classification: baseline

## Statement

For each container image promoted to production, organizations must generate and maintain an SBOM attestation that is cryptographically bound to the same image digest as the promoted artifact so consumers can verify that the software bill of materials applies to the exact deployed image.

## Objective

Ensure SBOM evidence remains tied to the exact artifact that is deployed so vulnerability, license, and component review decisions are made against the correct image digest rather than a detached or stale inventory record.

## Rationale

An SBOM that is not bound to the deployed image digest can be swapped, reused across different builds, or reviewed out of context. Digest-bound SBOM attestations preserve the integrity of component inventory evidence and make downstream policy and audit decisions defensible.

## Scope

Applies to all container images promoted to production and staging registries, including model-serving images, training images, and infrastructure operator images. Equivalent controls are required when managed registries or vendor tooling emit SBOM attestations on the organization's behalf.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: CI/CD artifact publication step and registry metadata attachment workflow.
- Implementation Surface: SBOM attestation generation and signing tooling, OCI referrers or equivalent registry attachment mechanisms, and digest-based verification workflows that confirm the SBOM attestation applies to the promoted image artifact.
- Owner Primary: DevSecOps / Release Engineering
- Owner Supporting: Security Engineering; Platform Engineering
