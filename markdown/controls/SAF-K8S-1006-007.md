# SAF-K8S-1006-007 - NIST SSDF v1.1 alignment and gap assessment for Kubernetes AI development

- Domain: D10 - Observability, Incident Response, and Governance
- Knowledge Area: 10.6 - Compliance and Governance
- Maturity: Advanced
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Organizations must map their Kubernetes and AI/ML development security practices to NIST SSDF v1.1 (SP 800-218) requirements and identify alignment and gaps across the Prepare, Protect the Software, Produce Well-Secured Software, and Respond to Vulnerabilities practice groups.

## Objective

Ensure secure development practices for Kubernetes AI workloads meet the NIST SSDF baseline and expose documented gaps that can be remediated through a defensible secure development program.

## Rationale

Without explicit SSDF alignment and gap assessment, organizations cannot show that their Kubernetes AI development practices meet federal secure software development expectations or maintain a consistent baseline across engineering teams and toolchains.

## Scope

Applies to all software development and ML engineering teams building or deploying AI workloads on Kubernetes. Covers the full NIST SSDF v1.1 practice group set and associated baseline secure development gap analysis.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Secure development policy documentation, CI/CD compliance checks, and SSDF self-attestation process.
- Implementation Surface: NIST SSDF v1.1 practice group alignment documentation, gap identification and remediation planning across PO, PS, PW, and RV practice groups, CI/CD compliance checks implementing SSDF baseline practices, and SSDF self-attestation or assessment workflows.
- Owner Primary: GRC/Compliance
- Owner Supporting: AI/ML Engineering; DevOps/Platform Engineering; Security Architecture
