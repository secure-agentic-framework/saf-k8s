# SAF-K8S-0905-050 - Authenticated reviewer identity validation for model promotion approvals

- Domain: D09 - AI Workload Security: Training, Serving, and Pipelines
- Knowledge Area: 9.5 - AI Supply Chain and Model Lifecycle
- Maturity: Advanced
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Model promotion approval workflows must require authenticated reviewer identity validation through approved identity mechanisms before an approval decision is accepted.

## Objective

Ensure that human promotion approvals are performed only by authenticated and authorized reviewers using trusted identity mechanisms.

## Rationale

If reviewer identity is not authenticated at approval time, approval actions can be forged, replayed, or submitted by unauthorized personnel, undermining the trustworthiness of human production-promotion decisions.

## Scope

Applies to all model promotion workflows requiring human approval on Kubernetes. Covers reviewer authentication, session-bound approval actions, and trusted identity provider integration for approval decisions. Excludes downstream audit-record binding of the approval decision to the promoted model version.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Approval workflow identity verification at decision time.
- Implementation Surface: Promotion approval workflows integrated with SSO or OIDC, authenticated approval actions, session-bound reviewer approvals, and authorization checks for approved reviewers.
- Owner Primary: Governance
- Owner Supporting: ML Platform Engineering; Security Engineering
