# SAF-K8S-0906-006 - Training data provenance tracking from ingestion through model training

- Domain: D09 - AI Workload Security: Training, Serving, and Pipelines
- Knowledge Area: 9.6 - Training Data Integrity and Poisoning Defense
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Organizations must maintain end-to-end provenance tracking for training data from ingestion through feature engineering to model training so that the origin and downstream use of training data can be traced.

## Objective

Establish verifiable lineage for training data so that compromised, biased, or recalled data can be traced to impacted models and pipeline outputs.

## Rationale

Without provenance tracking, organizations cannot determine which data was used to train a specific model or which models were affected when a data source is compromised, biased, or subject to regulatory recall.

## Scope

Applies to all training data pipelines on Kubernetes, from initial data ingestion through feature engineering to model training input. Covers data lineage metadata, provenance records, manifests, and queryability across pipeline stages. Excludes transformation-stage integrity verification controls.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Data lineage metadata store and provenance query workflow.
- Implementation Surface: Data lineage metadata stores recording source, timestamp, and lineage identifiers at ingestion and across pipeline stages, immutable provenance manifests, and provenance query tooling for tracing production models back to source data.
- Owner Primary: Data Engineering
- Owner Supporting: ML Platform Engineering; Security Engineering
