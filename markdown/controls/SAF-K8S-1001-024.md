# SAF-K8S-1001-024 - Audit log append-only storage and tamper protection

- Domain: D10 - Observability, Incident Response, and Governance
- Knowledge Area: 10.1 - Logging and Audit
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: baseline

## Statement

Audit logs must be stored with append-only or equivalent integrity protections that prevent unauthorized deletion, alteration, or silent overwriting of retained audit records.

## Objective

Preserve the integrity and evidentiary value of audit logs by enforcing storage-side protections against tampering.

## Rationale

Audit records only support investigation and compliance when their integrity can be trusted. Append-only storage, immutability controls, and restricted write paths prevent insiders or compromised administrators from erasing or rewriting evidence of malicious activity.

## Scope

Applies to centralized logging platforms, SIEM repositories, immutable storage backends, and archival systems receiving Kubernetes or AI workload audit data.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Audit log storage configuration and access controls enforcing append-only or immutable log retention.
- Implementation Surface: Object lock or immutability settings, write-only logging credentials, storage integrity controls, and RBAC preventing direct mutation of retained audit logs.
- Owner Primary: Security Operations
- Owner Supporting: GRC/Compliance; Identity and Access Management
