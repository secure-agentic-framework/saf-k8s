# SAF-K8S-0403-022 - Orphaned AI workload credential detection and remediation

- Domain: D04 - Identity, Access, and Secrets Management
- Knowledge Area: 4.3 - Secrets Management
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Organizations running AI workloads at scale must detect and remediate orphaned credentials left behind by completed or failed jobs.

## Objective

Ensure credentials are reconciled against workload state so abandoned secrets, tokens, and leases do not remain active after workload completion or failure.

## Rationale

Credential reconciliation is independently auditable from credential inventory. This control isolates orphaned credential detection and remediation so teams can verify that completed or failed AI jobs do not leave active credentials behind.

## Scope

Applies to all credentials issued to AI and ML workloads including Vault dynamic secrets, cloud IAM temporary credentials, Kubernetes service account tokens, API keys for external services, and model registry authentication tokens. Covers orphaned credential detection, job-state correlation, and remediation workflows. Inventory collection remains governed by SAF-K8S-0403-021.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Credential reconciliation engine and orphan-remediation workflow tied to workload state.
- Implementation Surface: Workload lifecycle correlation, orphaned lease or token scans, remediation workflows, and credential revocation or deletion automation tied to completed or failed AI jobs.
- Owner Primary: Security Engineering
- Owner Supporting: Platform Engineering; ML Engineering; Cloud Platform Engineering
