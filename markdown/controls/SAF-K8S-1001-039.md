# SAF-K8S-1001-039 - Durable retrieval validation for centralized AI logs

- Domain: D10 - Observability, Incident Response, and Governance
- Knowledge Area: 10.1 - Logging and Audit
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: baseline

## Statement

Organizations must verify that retained centralized AI workload logs remain durably retrievable for audit and investigation within the required retention window.

## Objective

Ensure retained centralized AI logs can still be retrieved when needed for investigation, audit, or regulatory review.

## Rationale

Retention settings alone are insufficient if retained logs cannot actually be recovered when auditors or responders need them. Durable retrieval validation confirms that retained centralized AI log evidence remains practically available throughout the retention window.

## Scope

Applies to centralized logging backends and retained AI workload log classes, including audit logs, training and inference logs, GPU hardware logs, and platform security telemetry retained for operational or regulatory purposes. Covers periodic validation that retained logs remain available and retrievable within the required retention window.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Durable retrieval validation procedures for centralized log evidence.
- Implementation Surface: Sample retrieval checks, durable recovery validation for retained log classes, and evidence that centralized AI workload logs remain available for audit and investigation during the required retention period.
- Owner Primary: Security Operations
- Owner Supporting: Platform Engineering; Infrastructure Engineering
