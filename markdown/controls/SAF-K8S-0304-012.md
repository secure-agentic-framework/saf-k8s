# SAF-K8S-0304-012 - Training scratch volume size limits and ephemeral-storage quotas

- Domain: D03 - Workload and Pod Security
- Knowledge Area: 3.4 - Secure Defaults and Resource Constraints
- Maturity: Foundational
- Classification: baseline

## Statement

AI training workloads that use scratch volumes must enforce emptyDir sizeLimit constraints, and training namespaces must enforce ephemeral-storage quota or equivalent policy boundaries so scratch-space growth cannot exhaust node or namespace storage capacity.

## Objective

Prevent disk exhaustion and training job runaway behavior by bounding scratch-volume size and aggregate ephemeral-storage consumption.

## Rationale

Training jobs generate large caches, tokenized samples, and intermediate artifacts that can rapidly consume local disk. emptyDir size limits bound individual scratch volumes, while namespace ephemeral-storage quotas prevent a team or job set from consuming all available storage across the cluster.

## Scope

Applies to AI and ML training workloads that use emptyDir or equivalent ephemeral scratch space for staging data, caches, gradients, or intermediate outputs.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Pod specification validation and namespace quota enforcement for emptyDir size limits and ephemeral-storage ceilings.
- Implementation Surface: Pod spec emptyDir sizeLimit settings, ephemeral-storage requests and limits, and namespace quota or policy objects governing scratch-space consumption.
- Owner Primary: AI/ML Platform team
- Owner Supporting: Platform Engineering; Security Engineering
