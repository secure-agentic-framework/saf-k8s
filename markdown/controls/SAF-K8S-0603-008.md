# SAF-K8S-0603-008 - Build materials and artifact attestations per NIST SP 800-204D

- Domain: D06 - Supply Chain, Images, and Admission Control
- Knowledge Area: 6.3 - Attestation, Provenance, and Cryptographic Assurance
- Maturity: Advanced
- Classification: baseline

## Statement

Build pipelines must generate independently signed and verifiable materials attestations capturing all source inputs and dependencies consumed and artifacts attestations identifying build outputs and intermediate products, in alignment with NIST SP 800-204D.

## Objective

Ensure build inputs and resulting outputs are independently attested so dependency substitution, source tampering, and output provenance gaps are detectable.

## Rationale

Materials and artifact attestations provide the provenance needed to verify what inputs entered the build and what outputs were produced. Without them, dependency substitution and output tampering can remain invisible even when the build platform itself is trusted.

## Scope

Applies to all CI/CD pipelines that produce container images or deployable artifacts for production clusters, including pipelines that generate intermediate OCI artifacts, Helm charts, and related release outputs.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: CI/CD attestation generation for consumed materials and produced artifacts.
- Implementation Surface: Materials attestation for source and dependency inputs, artifacts attestation for build outputs and intermediate products, per-category signatures, and verification of materials and artifact attestation presence.
- Owner Primary: DevSecOps / Release Engineering
- Owner Supporting: Security Engineering; Platform Engineering
