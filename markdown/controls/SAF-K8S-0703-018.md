# SAF-K8S-0703-018 - GPU cost attribution metering and billing correlation

- Domain: D07 - Storage, Multi-tenancy, and Resource Governance
- Knowledge Area: 7.3 - Resource Governance and Priority
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Organizations must measure and attribute GPU consumption at the namespace, team, and job level by correlating Kubernetes workload metadata with billing and utilization telemetry.

## Objective

Provide granular, trustworthy visibility into GPU consumption so organizations can identify which namespaces, teams, and jobs are responsible for incurred GPU spend.

## Rationale

Without reliable attribution, organizations cannot determine which workloads are driving GPU cost, cannot distinguish productive consumption from waste, and cannot make defensible budget, capacity, or optimization decisions for expensive AI infrastructure.

## Scope

Applies to all Kubernetes clusters with GPU resources. Attribution must cover per-namespace, per-team, and per-job granularity and must align Kubernetes labels or namespaces with cloud billing or equivalent cost telemetry sources.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: GPU monitoring and cost attribution tooling integrated with billing and workload metadata sources.
- Implementation Surface: Cloud billing tags or labels mapped to Kubernetes namespace, team, project, and job dimensions, plus cost monitoring tools that calculate attributable GPU spend from workload and utilization telemetry.
- Owner Primary: FinOps
- Owner Supporting: Platform Engineering; ML Engineering
