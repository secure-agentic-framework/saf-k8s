# SAF-K8S-0403-027 - Secret leak incident triage and containment workflow

- Domain: D04 - Identity, Access, and Secrets Management
- Knowledge Area: 4.3 - Secrets Management
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: baseline

## Statement

Organizations must implement incident response procedures for secret leak events that drive rapid triage, containment, escalation, and documented response actions when leaked secrets are detected.

## Objective

Ensure detected secret leaks trigger a defined response workflow that contains exposure quickly and records the incident for follow-up.

## Rationale

Incident triage and containment are independently auditable from the credential lifecycle actions that revoke or replace the exposed secret. This control isolates the response workflow so teams can verify that leak findings trigger timely containment, escalation, and incident documentation.

## Scope

Applies to any detected exposure of credentials, tokens, keys, or secrets in repositories, images, CI/CD artifacts, logs, and runtime outputs. Covers response initiation, containment, escalation, and incident documentation. Credential revocation and replacement remain governed by SAF-K8S-0403-028.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Incident response workflow triggered by secret leak findings.
- Implementation Surface: Secret leak triage workflows, containment actions, notification and escalation paths, and incident records showing response timing and handling.
- Owner Primary: Security Engineering
- Owner Supporting: Platform Engineering; Application Development
