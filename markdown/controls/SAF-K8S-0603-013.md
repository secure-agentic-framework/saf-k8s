# SAF-K8S-0603-013 - Lifecycle attestation chain verification across build, promote, and deploy

- Domain: D06 - Supply Chain, Images, and Admission Control
- Knowledge Area: 6.3 - Attestation, Provenance, and Cryptographic Assurance
- Maturity: Advanced
- Classification: baseline

## Statement

Organizations must verify attestation chains at each required lifecycle transition, including build, promote, and deploy, before artifacts advance to the next approved stage.

## Objective

Ensure attestation checks are executed consistently across artifact lifecycle transitions so incomplete, invalid, or stale attestation chains are detected before downstream use.

## Rationale

Attestation policies do not reduce supply chain risk unless every required lifecycle transition actually verifies the attestation chain before promotion, distribution, or deployment proceeds. Lifecycle verification provides the evidence that attestation requirements were evaluated when artifacts moved downstream.

## Scope

Applies to all CI/CD pipelines, artifact promotion workflows, and deployment workflows that process signed artifact attestations from build through deployment.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: CI/CD and promotion gate verification steps at lifecycle transition points.
- Implementation Surface: Policy engine evaluation within build, promotion, and deploy workflows; attestation chain verification checks at lifecycle transitions; and pass/fail logs showing whether artifacts satisfied attestation requirements before advancing.
- Owner Primary: Security Engineering
- Owner Supporting: DevSecOps / Release Engineering; Platform Engineering
