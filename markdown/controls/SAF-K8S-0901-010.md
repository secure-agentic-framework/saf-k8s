# SAF-K8S-0901-010 - Encrypted inter-worker gradient transport

- Domain: D09 - AI Workload Security: Training, Serving, and Pipelines
- Knowledge Area: 9.1 - Distributed Training Workload Security
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Multi-node distributed training jobs must protect inter-worker gradient communication with encrypted transport where the communication framework supports it.

## Objective

Protect distributed training traffic against eavesdropping and in-transit tampering by enabling framework-supported encryption for gradient exchange.

## Rationale

Gradient traffic can reveal model internals, training data characteristics, or proprietary model updates. When frameworks support encrypted transport, operators must enable it to reduce exposure to interception or tampering on the cluster or fabric network.

## Scope

Applies to multi-node distributed training workloads using NCCL, MPI, TensorFlow gRPC, or equivalent transports that support encrypted inter-worker communication.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Training framework and transport configuration controlling encrypted worker-to-worker communication.
- Implementation Surface: NCCL encryption settings, MPI transport encryption, TensorFlow gRPC TLS, and related certificate or key distribution supporting encrypted gradient exchange.
- Owner Primary: ML Platform Engineering
- Owner Supporting: Security Engineering; Network Engineering
