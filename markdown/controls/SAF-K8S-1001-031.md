# SAF-K8S-1001-031 - Tenant and workload context preservation for AI log segregation and searchability

- Domain: D10 - Observability, Incident Response, and Governance
- Knowledge Area: 10.1 - Logging and Audit
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Organizations must preserve source, tenant, and workload context on centralized AI workload logs so those log streams remain searchable, segregated, and usable for investigation and governance.

## Objective

Ensure centralized AI workload logs retain the source, tenant, and workload context needed for segregation, search, and investigative use.

## Rationale

Centralized logging is not sufficient if AI workload logs lose tenant or workload context after collection. Without workload-aware labels and preserved metadata, multi-tenant platforms cannot separate one tenant's activity from another's or reliably reconstruct events for investigation.

## Scope

Applies to all centralized AI workload logs on Kubernetes. Covers workload metadata enrichment, tenant-aware segregation, searchable source labeling, and retained context for investigation and governance. Excludes the initial collection and routing of AI workload logs into the enterprise platform.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Workload metadata enrichment and search or index segregation configuration.
- Implementation Surface: Workload and tenant metadata enrichment; centralized logging platform indexes, views, or labels that preserve source, tenant, and workload context; and searchable segregation of AI workload log classes for investigation.
- Owner Primary: Security Operations
- Owner Supporting: AI/ML Engineering; Data Privacy
