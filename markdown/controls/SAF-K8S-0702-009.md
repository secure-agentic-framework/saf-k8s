# SAF-K8S-0702-009 - Admission control enforcement of workload classification label requirements

- Domain: D07 - Storage, Multi-tenancy, and Resource Governance
- Knowledge Area: 7.2 - Namespace Isolation and Multi-Tenancy
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Organizations must enforce the standardized workload classification labeling schema through admission control by configuring admission webhooks or admission policies to reject workloads that do not carry the required classification labels, preventing unlabeled workloads from bypassing governance policies.

## Objective

Ensure that all AI workloads entering the cluster carry required classification labels by using admission control as a mandatory enforcement gate that rejects non-compliant workloads before scheduling.

## Rationale

Admission control enforcement prevents unlabeled workloads from bypassing governance policies. Without admission control, the labeling schema defined in SAF-K8S-0702-005 cannot be reliably enforced, as workloads may be submitted without the required classification labels and still run, undermining policy enforcement, cost attribution, and security controls. Admission control provides the mechanism that makes the labeling schema operationally binding.

## Scope

Applies to all pods, jobs, deployments, and statefulsets in namespaces designated for AI workloads where admission control enforcement of classification labels is required.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Admission control policies (OPA Gatekeeper, Kyverno) that validate required labels on pod creation, and namespace-level label enforcement through validating webhooks.
- Implementation Surface: Kubernetes admission control policy engines (Kyverno ClusterPolicy, OPA/Gatekeeper ConstraintTemplates) configured to validate required label presence on pods in AI namespaces, validating webhooks that reject pod creation when required classification labels are absent or carry invalid values.
- Owner Primary: Platform Engineering
- Owner Supporting: ML Engineering; Security Engineering
