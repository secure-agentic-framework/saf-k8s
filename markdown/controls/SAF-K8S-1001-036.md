# SAF-K8S-1001-036 - Regulatory audit log retention period configuration and compliance verification

- Domain: D10 - Observability, Incident Response, and Governance
- Knowledge Area: 10.1 - Logging and Audit
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Organizations subject to regulatory requirements must configure audit log storage systems with retention periods that meet or exceed applicable minimum regulatory durations and must use automated compliance checks to detect under-configured retention periods.

## Objective

Ensure regulatory audit logs for Kubernetes AI workloads are configured with retention periods that satisfy applicable minimum regulatory durations and are continuously checked for under-configuration.

## Rationale

Regulatory frameworks impose minimum audit log retention periods. If retention durations are under-configured and not detected, organizations lose evidence needed for compliance audits, incident investigations, and post-market monitoring even when centralized log retention mechanisms otherwise function.

## Scope

Applies to audit log storage systems receiving Kubernetes and AI workload audit data subject to regulatory retention requirements. Covers retention-period configuration and automated compliance verification against applicable minimum durations. Excludes immutable deletion prevention and WORM enforcement mechanics.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Audit log storage retention period policy configuration and compliance checking.
- Implementation Surface: Object storage retention period settings, log management system retention configuration, SIEM retention policy values, regulatory retention mapping documentation, and automated scans that identify under-configured audit log retention periods.
- Owner Primary: GRC/Compliance
- Owner Supporting: Platform Engineering; Security Engineering
