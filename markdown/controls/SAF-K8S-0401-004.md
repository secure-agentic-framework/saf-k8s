# SAF-K8S-0401-004 - RBAC for AI operator custom resources

- Domain: D04 - Identity, Access, and Secrets Management
- Knowledge Area: 4.1 - Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
- Maturity: Foundational
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Organizations must define and enforce dedicated RBAC policies for AI operator custom resource definitions including InferenceService, TrainingJob, RayCluster, and PodGroup, restricting create, update, and delete operations to authorized ML engineering and platform roles.

## Objective

Ensure that access to AI-specific custom resources is explicitly governed by RBAC, preventing unauthorized users from deploying, modifying, or deleting AI workloads through operator CRDs.

## Rationale

AI operator CRDs such as InferenceService, TrainingJob, RayCluster, and PodGroup control the deployment of GPU-intensive workloads, model serving endpoints, and distributed training clusters. Without dedicated RBAC policies for these custom resources, any user with broad namespace permissions could deploy unauthorized models, consume expensive GPU resources, or tamper with training pipelines. Default RBAC often does not cover custom resources explicitly.

## Scope

Applies to all clusters running AI/ML operators such as KServe, Kubeflow Training Operator, KubeRay, or Volcano. Covers all custom resource types registered by these operators. Must be enforced in namespaces where ML workloads are scheduled.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Namespace-scoped Roles and RoleBindings for AI operator CRDs, with admission policies preventing creation of these resources by unauthorized subjects.
- Implementation Surface: Namespace-scoped Roles and RoleBindings for AI operator CRDs (KServe InferenceService, Kubeflow TrainingJob/PyTorchJob/TFJob/MPIJob, KubeRay RayCluster, Volcano PodGroup), admission policies restricting CRD create/update/delete to authorized ML engineering and platform groups, and Kubernetes audit logs for denied access attempts against AI custom resources.
- Owner Primary: Platform Engineering
- Owner Supporting: ML Engineering; Security Engineering
