# SAF-K8S-1006-009 - Continuous policy decision evidence generation and export for AI workload compliance

- Domain: D10 - Observability, Incident Response, and Governance
- Knowledge Area: 10.6 - Compliance and Governance
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Organizations must continuously generate, retain, and export compliance evidence from policy-as-code enforcement decisions for Kubernetes AI workloads, including policy reports, admission allow and deny events, and policy version history.

## Objective

Produce audit-ready evidence of policy enforcement outcomes so compliance and control effectiveness can be demonstrated without manual reconstruction.

## Rationale

Without retained decision evidence and policy history, organizations cannot prove that policy-as-code controls were operating as intended at the time of deployment or admission. Continuous evidence generation supports audit readiness, investigations, and ongoing governance assurance.

## Scope

Applies to policy-enforced Kubernetes AI workloads and the associated evidence pipelines that capture, retain, and export policy decision records.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Policy reporting pipelines, admission decision logs, and evidence export integrations.
- Implementation Surface: PolicyReport and Gatekeeper audit outputs, admission controller decision logs, policy source control history, SIEM exports, and GRC evidence repositories.
- Owner Primary: GRC/Compliance
- Owner Supporting: Platform Engineering; AI/ML Engineering; Security Engineering
