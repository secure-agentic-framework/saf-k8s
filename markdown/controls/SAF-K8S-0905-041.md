# SAF-K8S-0905-041 - Model registry sensitive-operation alerting and anomalous activity review

- Domain: D09 - AI Workload Security: Training, Serving, and Pipelines
- Knowledge Area: 9.5 - AI Supply Chain and Model Lifecycle
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Organizations must alert on sensitive model registry operations and perform operational review of anomalous publish, pull, deletion, permission-change, and administrative activity to support investigation and response.

## Objective

Provide accountable detection and triage for suspicious registry behavior by reviewing sensitive registry operations and anomalous activity.

## Rationale

Generating and forwarding audit events is insufficient if no one monitors them. Alerting and operational review are required to detect model disclosure, tampering, or abuse patterns in time to investigate and respond.

## Scope

Applies to all model registry deployments on Kubernetes, including Harbor, MLflow Model Registry, and on-premises Hugging Face Hub instances. Covers alerting and operational review of suspicious registry activity after audit events have been captured and forwarded.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Centralized monitoring review workflow and alert handling for sensitive registry operations.
- Implementation Surface: Alerts for sensitive registry actions, periodic review of anomalous registry operations, analyst investigation workflow, and review records for suspicious publish, pull, deletion, and permission-change activity.
- Owner Primary: ML Platform Engineering
- Owner Supporting: Security Engineering; Platform Engineering
