# SAF-K8S-0402-014 - Ephemeral training job credential revocation on completion

- Domain: D04 - Identity, Access, and Secrets Management
- Knowledge Area: 4.2 - Service Accounts and Workload Identity
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Credentials issued to ephemeral training jobs must be revoked or cleaned up upon job completion or failure.

## Objective

Ensure training job credentials are explicitly terminated when the associated job ends, eliminating orphaned credentials that could be exploited after job termination even if TTL has not yet expired.

## Rationale

Even when credentials have TTL alignment, explicit revocation on job completion provides defense-in-depth. Job failures in particular may leave credentials that have not yet expired. Without explicit cleanup, credential accumulation across thousands of training runs creates an expanding attack surface from orphaned secrets.

## Scope

Applies to all credentials issued to ephemeral training jobs including Kubernetes service account tokens, cloud IAM short-lived credentials, Vault dynamic secrets, and any other credentials provisioned at job start.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Kubernetes Job finalizers and post-completion hooks, Vault lease revocation on job termination, and cloud IAM credential revocation mechanisms triggered by job lifecycle events.
- Implementation Surface: Kubernetes Job finalizers for credential revocation, post-completion cleanup hooks, Vault lease revocation API calls, cloud IAM credential invalidation on job termination, and orphaned credential scanning for completed training runs.
- Owner Primary: ML Engineering
- Owner Supporting: Platform Engineering; Security Engineering
