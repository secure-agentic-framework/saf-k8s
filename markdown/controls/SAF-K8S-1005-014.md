# SAF-K8S-1005-014 - Ransomware recovery prioritization and post-incident preparedness improvement

- Domain: D10 - Observability, Incident Response, and Governance
- Knowledge Area: 10.5 - Incident Response for Kubernetes
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: baseline

## Statement

Organizations must prioritize Kubernetes workload recovery based on business criticality during ransomware or destructive attack response and must conduct post-incident lessons-learned processes that feed preparedness improvements back into response playbooks, containment procedures, and related recovery planning.

## Objective

Ensure ransomware and destructive-attack recovery for Kubernetes environments is governed by business-priority restoration decisions and structured post-incident improvement.

## Rationale

Validated backups alone do not minimize business impact if critical AI inference services are restored after lower-priority workloads or if lessons from the incident never improve future readiness. Recovery prioritization and post-incident improvement are separate governance obligations that shape response outcomes and preparedness maturity.

## Scope

Applies to production Kubernetes clusters and their AI workloads during ransomware or destructive attack response. Covers workload recovery prioritization by business criticality and post-incident lessons-learned processes with tracked preparedness improvements. Excludes backup verification and restoration test execution mechanics.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Disaster recovery prioritization framework and post-incident review process.
- Implementation Surface: Workload recovery prioritization matrices for control plane, logging, monitoring, inference, training, and pipeline infrastructure; business criticality criteria for restoration order; post-incident lessons-learned reports; and tracked improvement actions feeding back into playbooks, containment procedures, and audit policy updates.
- Owner Primary: Security Operations
- Owner Supporting: Platform Engineering; SRE; AI/ML Engineering
