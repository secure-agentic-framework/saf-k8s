# SAF-K8S-0905-053 - Approved external model source periodic review and allowlist update governance

- Domain: D09 - AI Workload Security: Training, Serving, and Pipelines
- Knowledge Area: 9.5 - AI Supply Chain and Model Lifecycle
- Maturity: Advanced
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Organizations must periodically review approved external model source allowlists and update or remove entries when publishers, repositories, or trust conditions no longer meet organizational approval requirements.

## Objective

Ensure that approved external model source allowlists remain current through periodic review, documented updates, and removal of stale or no-longer-trusted sources.

## Rationale

A source allowlist degrades over time if it is never revalidated. Periodic review reduces exposure to compromised publishers, ownership changes, abandoned repositories, and other trust shifts that can make previously approved model sources unsafe.

## Scope

Applies to all external model source allowlists used from Kubernetes infrastructure. Covers periodic review cadence, review records, update decisions, and removal or modification of stale or no-longer-trusted external sources.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Allowlist review cadence and update decision process.
- Implementation Surface: Review records showing periodic revalidation of approved external model sources, update records for additions or removals, and governance evidence showing stale or risky sources are removed when approval conditions change.
- Owner Primary: Security Engineering
- Owner Supporting: ML Platform Engineering; Governance
