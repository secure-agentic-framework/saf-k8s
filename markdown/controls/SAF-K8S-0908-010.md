# SAF-K8S-0908-010 - Inference privacy budget tracking and threshold enforcement

- Domain: D09 - AI Workload Security: Training, Serving, and Pipelines
- Knowledge Area: 9.8 - Model Abuse and Extraction Prevention
- Maturity: Advanced
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Inference endpoints serving models trained on sensitive data must track cumulative privacy budget consumption per model or principal and enforce privacy-loss thresholds before additional responses are served.

## Objective

Enforce cumulative privacy budget accounting and cutoffs for repeated access to sensitive inference endpoints.

## Rationale

Even when differential privacy parameters are well chosen, repeated queries can exhaust the intended privacy guarantee. Without budget accounting and threshold enforcement, privacy loss accumulates silently and the formal protection bound becomes meaningless in practice.

## Scope

Applies to Kubernetes-hosted inference endpoints serving models trained on sensitive data where formal differential privacy guarantees are required. Covers cumulative privacy budget accounting, spend monitoring, threshold enforcement, and request blocking or equivalent control once the allowable privacy loss is exhausted. Excludes initial parameter selection and guarantee documentation.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Privacy budget accounting middleware and threshold enforcement logic for inference endpoints.
- Implementation Surface: Per-principal or per-model privacy budget accounting middleware, cumulative spend monitoring, threshold enforcement logic, and request blocking or equivalent controls once privacy-loss limits are reached.
- Owner Primary: Security Engineering
- Owner Supporting: ML Platform Engineering; Privacy Engineering
