# SAF-K8S-1007-013 - Automated AI infrastructure asset discovery and continuously updated inventory

- Domain: D10 - Observability, Incident Response, and Governance
- Knowledge Area: 10.7 - Cluster Lifecycle and Asset Inventory
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Organizations must maintain an automated, continuously updated inventory of AI infrastructure assets running on Kubernetes, including models, datasets, ML pipelines, inference endpoints, and GPU allocations.

## Objective

Provide current visibility into AI assets on Kubernetes so security, operations, and governance teams can discover what exists and where it resides.

## Rationale

Without automated discovery and current inventory records, organizations lose track of deployed models, datasets, pipelines, endpoints, and GPU allocations. That creates blind spots for security monitoring, incident response, and operational ownership.

## Scope

Applies to Kubernetes clusters hosting AI workloads and covers automated discovery and inventory maintenance for AI infrastructure assets.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Asset inventory automation tooling, Kubernetes resource discovery agents, and registry or metadata integrations.
- Implementation Surface: Automated asset discovery controllers, model registry integrations, PVC and object storage discovery, GPU allocation tracking, asset registry storage, and reconciliation workflows.
- Owner Primary: Platform Engineering
- Owner Supporting: AI/ML Engineering; Security Operations; GRC/Compliance
