# SAF-K8S-1006-010 - Policy exception approval and expiration governance for AI workload compliance

- Domain: D10 - Observability, Incident Response, and Governance
- Knowledge Area: 10.6 - Compliance and Governance
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Organizations must document AI workload compliance policy exceptions with approver identity, justification, scope, and expiration metadata, and must track review, renewal, or closure of those exceptions before they lapse or become permanent bypasses.

## Objective

Ensure policy deviations are explicitly approved, time-bounded, and auditable so exceptions do not silently erode compliance enforcement.

## Rationale

Policy exceptions create unmanaged compliance and security drift when they are undocumented, lack accountable approval, or remain open indefinitely. Formal exception governance preserves oversight, compensating-control review, and expiry discipline.

## Scope

Applies to exceptions granted against AI workload compliance policies enforced through policy-as-code and related governance workflows.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Exception approval workflows, exception registers, and periodic governance review.
- Implementation Surface: Exception registers, approval tickets, compensating control documentation, expiration tracking, and periodic review workflows.
- Owner Primary: GRC/Compliance
- Owner Supporting: Platform Engineering; AI/ML Engineering; Security Engineering
