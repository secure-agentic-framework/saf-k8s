# SAF-K8S-0402-012 - Ephemeral training job credential expiration

- Domain: D04 - Identity, Access, and Secrets Management
- Knowledge Area: 4.2 - Service Accounts and Workload Identity
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Credentials issued to ephemeral training jobs must be short-lived with expiration aligned to the expected job duration.

## Objective

Ensure credentials for ephemeral training jobs expire when the workload completes, preventing stale credentials from persisting after job termination through natural TTL-based expiry.

## Rationale

Short-lived credentials with duration-aligned expiration prevent orphaned secrets when training jobs complete normally. TTL alignment eliminates a class of credential accumulation risk without requiring explicit revocation infrastructure for every credential type.

## Scope

Applies to all credential types issued to ephemeral training jobs including Kubernetes service account tokens, cloud IAM short-lived credentials, Vault dynamic secrets, and database session tokens.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Projected ServiceAccountToken expirationSeconds, Vault dynamic secret TTL configuration, and cloud workload identity federation token validity periods.
- Implementation Surface: Projected ServiceAccountToken expirationSeconds aligned to job duration, Vault dynamic secret TTLs (1-4 hour leases), and cloud workload identity federation short-lived credentials with TTL matching estimated job runtime.
- Owner Primary: ML Engineering
- Owner Supporting: Platform Engineering; Security Engineering
