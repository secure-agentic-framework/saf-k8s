# SAF-K8S-0402-022 - Projected service account token lifetime bounds enforcement

- Domain: D04 - Identity, Access, and Secrets Management
- Knowledge Area: 4.2 - Service Accounts and Workload Identity
- Maturity: Foundational
- Classification: baseline

## Statement

Kubernetes clusters must enforce time-bounded projected service account token lifetimes through bounded expiration settings and policy validation so workload tokens are issued only for the minimum practical duration.

## Objective

Reduce the replay window for workload identity tokens by enforcing short projected-token lifetimes.

## Rationale

Even when issuance paths and audiences are correctly scoped, a stolen workload token remains useful until it expires. Enforcing bounded projected-token lifetimes reduces the value of token theft and limits the duration of any replay opportunity.

## Scope

Applies to all projected workload service account tokens used for in-cluster or federated workload authentication.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Token lifetime policy enforcement and validation of projected token expiration settings.
- Implementation Surface: Projected token expirationSeconds settings, policy validation, and workload token lifetime audit evidence.
- Owner Primary: Platform Engineering
- Owner Supporting: Security Engineering; Application Development
