# SAF-K8S-0405-017 - Credential policy baseline requirements

- Domain: D04 - Identity, Access, and Secrets Management
- Knowledge Area: 4.5 - Identity Abuse Detection and Mitigation
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: baseline

## Statement

Organizations must enforce credential policy baseline requirements including credential complexity standards for Kubernetes cluster and AI infrastructure authentication pathways.

## Objective

Ensure authentication credentials meet baseline strength requirements so weak credentials do not become an initial access path into the cluster.

## Rationale

Credential policy requirements are independently auditable from the secure storage and lifecycle handling of those credentials. This control isolates the baseline policy claim so teams can verify that credential strength requirements are actually enforced in the identity systems used for cluster and AI infrastructure access.

## Scope

Applies to authentication pathways for Kubernetes clusters, OIDC integrations, and cloud provider IAM integrations that rely on managed credentials. Covers credential complexity and baseline policy requirements. Secure storage and lifecycle handling remain governed by SAF-K8S-0405-018.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Credential policy controls in identity providers and authentication systems.
- Implementation Surface: Credential complexity and baseline policy settings in OIDC providers, cloud IAM, and cluster authentication systems.
- Owner Primary: Identity and Access Management
- Owner Supporting: Platform Engineering; Security Engineering
