# SAF-K8S-0601-011 - Integrity metadata co-retention with software artifacts

- Domain: D06 - Supply Chain, Images, and Admission Control
- Knowledge Area: 6.1 - Container Image and Registry Security
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: baseline

## Statement

Organizations must retain integrity verification data such as signatures, attestations, SBOMs, and related OCI referrers for at least as long as the artifact they describe, using lifecycle policies that prevent orphaning or premature deletion of integrity metadata.

## Objective

Ensure provenance and integrity evidence remains available alongside retained artifacts for audit, vulnerability investigation, and trust verification.

## Rationale

Retaining an artifact without its associated signature, attestation, or SBOM destroys the evidence needed to validate provenance and investigate vulnerabilities, while retaining metadata without the subject artifact creates incomplete and misleading records.

## Scope

All registries and artifact stores that hold integrity verification metadata for container images, Helm charts, ML model artifacts, or other OCI artifacts.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Lifecycle management for signatures, attestations, SBOMs, and related integrity metadata linked to stored artifacts.
- Implementation Surface: OCI referrer retention policy, signature and attestation retention rules, SBOM storage lifecycle controls, and metadata cleanup logic that tracks the lifecycle of the subject artifact.
- Owner Primary: Platform Engineering
- Owner Supporting: DevSecOps / Release Engineering; ML Platform Engineering
