# SAF-K8S-0403-029 - Environment variable secret injection prohibition enforcement

- Domain: D04 - Identity, Access, and Secrets Management
- Knowledge Area: 4.3 - Secrets Management
- Maturity: Foundational
- Classification: baseline

## Statement

Organizations must prohibit injection of secrets via environment variables for production workloads by enforcing admission policies that detect and reject Pod specifications containing environment variable references to Secret objects.

## Objective

Prevent secrets from being exposed through environment variable injection by enforcing a production-wide prohibition at admission time.

## Rationale

The actual enforcement of an environment-variable prohibition is independently auditable from the governance of any rare exceptions. This control isolates the blocking requirement so teams can verify that production workloads cannot reference Secret objects through environment variables under normal policy.

## Scope

Applies to all production workloads in Kubernetes clusters. Covers admission enforcement for environment variable Secret references and enforcement-mode configuration by namespace or environment. Approved deviations and their review process remain governed by SAF-K8S-0403-030. Selection of approved alternative patterns remains governed by SAF-K8S-0403-003.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Admission enforcement for Secret reference patterns.
- Implementation Surface: Kyverno or OPA Gatekeeper policies rejecting environment variable Secret references, namespace enforcement configuration, and enforcement reports for blocked or denied attempts.
- Owner Primary: Application Development
- Owner Supporting: Security Engineering; Platform Engineering
