# SAF-K8S-0701-004 - PV reclaim policy enforcement for AI data volumes

- Domain: D07 - Storage, Multi-tenancy, and Resource Governance
- Knowledge Area: 7.1 - Persistent Storage Security
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Organizations must configure PersistentVolume reclaim policies to prevent data remnants after volume release, and must enforce the Retain policy for volumes containing training data backups and model checkpoint archives.

## Objective

Prevent unintended data loss of valuable AI artifacts through premature volume deletion and prevent data leakage through insecure volume reclamation.

## Rationale

The Delete reclaim policy automatically destroys the underlying storage when a PVC is released, which can cause irreversible loss of training data and model checkpoints that may have taken days or weeks of compute to produce. Conversely, if volumes are reclaimed without proper data wiping, residual data from a previous tenant's training run can be exposed to the next consumer. Dual authorization for destruction prevents a single compromised account from deleting critical AI artifacts.

## Scope

Applies to all persistent volumes in Kubernetes clusters hosting AI workloads, with mandatory Retain policy for volumes storing training data backups and model checkpoint archives. Dual authorization requirements apply to deletion requests for retained AI data volumes. Scratch volumes and ephemeral training caches may use Delete policy with documented justification.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: PersistentVolume reclaimPolicy settings, StorageClass reclaimPolicy defaults, and admission control policies enforcing Retain for labeled AI data volumes.
- Implementation Surface: PersistentVolume spec.persistentVolumeReclaimPolicy configuration (Retain, Delete), StorageClass reclaimPolicy defaults, PV metadata labels for data-classification and retention-policy, admission webhooks enforcing Retain on AI data volumes, and cloud provider secure erasure verification.
- Owner Primary: Platform Engineering
- Owner Supporting: ML Engineering; Security Engineering; Data Governance
