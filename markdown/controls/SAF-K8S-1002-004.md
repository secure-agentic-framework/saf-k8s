# SAF-K8S-1002-004 - AI-specific alerting and failure mode detection

- Domain: D10 - Observability, Incident Response, and Governance
- Knowledge Area: 10.2 - Monitoring, Metrics, and Tracing
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Organizations must configure alerts for AI-specific failure modes including training divergence, model staleness, GPU hardware errors, inference accuracy degradation, inference latency spikes, and training job hangs, with alert routing to security operations for conditions that may indicate security incidents.

## Objective

Proactively detect AI workload anomalies that may indicate security incidents, data poisoning, supply chain compromise, or operational degradation before business impact materializes.

## Rationale

Without AI-specific alerting, conditions such as training divergence (potential data poisoning), model staleness (potential supply chain compromise), or inference accuracy degradation (potential evasion attacks) go undetected until business impact materializes. These failure modes require domain-specific alert thresholds distinct from generic infrastructure monitoring.

## Scope

Applies to all AI workloads on Kubernetes including training jobs, inference services, and pipeline orchestrators. Covers alert rule definitions, thresholds, and routing for AI-specific failure modes. Complements SAF-K8S-1002-002 (distributed tracing).

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Alerting rule definitions in the monitoring stack (Prometheus Alertmanager, PagerDuty, Opsgenie) with AI-specific thresholds.
- Implementation Surface: Alerting rule definitions for training divergence (loss increasing over consecutive epochs), model staleness (time since last update exceeding threshold), GPU hardware errors (dcgm_fi_dev_xid_errors counter), inference accuracy degradation, inference latency spikes (p99 exceeding baseline), and training job hangs (no checkpoint within expected interval), with alert routing to security operations for security-relevant conditions.
- Owner Primary: Security Operations
- Owner Supporting: AI/ML Engineering; SRE
