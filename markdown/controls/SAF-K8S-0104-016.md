# SAF-K8S-0104-016 - CIS Benchmark remediation workflow tracking

- Domain: D01 - Control Plane and Cluster Hardening
- Knowledge Area: 1.4 - CIS Benchmarks and Patch Management
- Maturity: Foundational
- Classification: baseline

## Statement

Failing CIS Kubernetes Benchmark checks must be tracked through a documented remediation workflow with assigned ownership and defined resolution timelines so that benchmark findings produce accountable corrective action rather than unmanaged backlog.

## Objective

Ensure benchmark findings drive timely, accountable remediation through an auditable workflow with owners and due dates.

## Rationale

Benchmark scans do not improve security unless findings are converted into owned corrective actions. This control isolates the remediation workflow obligation so teams can independently verify that failing checks are assigned, tracked, and resolved on a defined timeline.

## Scope

Applies to all production clusters and to all failing CIS Benchmark checks identified through recurring assessment.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Workflow / Governance
- Implementation Surface: Issue tracking, remediation management, and ownership assignment workflow tied to CIS Benchmark findings.
- Owner Primary: Security Engineering
- Owner Supporting: Platform Engineering; Governance Risk and Compliance
