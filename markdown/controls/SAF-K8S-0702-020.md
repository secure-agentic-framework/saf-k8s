# SAF-K8S-0702-020 - Namespace ResourceQuota enforcement for GPU and AI workloads

- Domain: D07 - Storage, Multi-tenancy, and Resource Governance
- Knowledge Area: 7.2 - Namespace Isolation and Multi-Tenancy
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Namespace ResourceQuota objects SHALL enforce approved per-namespace limits for GPU, CPU, memory, storage, and relevant object counts for AI and accelerator-consuming workloads so that no tenant or workload can exceed its authorized share of cluster resources.

## Objective

Prevent any single namespace from exceeding its approved share of GPU and related cluster resources by enforcing namespace-level ResourceQuota boundaries for AI workloads.

## Rationale

GPU and AI workload capacity is scarce, expensive, and easy to exhaust. Namespace ResourceQuota enforcement prevents tenant monopolization, quota bypass through uncontrolled namespace growth, and cross-tenant disruption caused by runaway training or inference workloads.

## Scope

Applies to AI or accelerator-consuming namespaces where GPU, CPU, memory, storage, and relevant object-count ceilings must be enforced through ResourceQuota. LimitRange defaults remain a separate companion control.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Namespace ResourceQuota objects enforced by the Kubernetes resource quota admission controller and validated through periodic configuration review.
- Implementation Surface: ResourceQuota objects specifying requests and limits for CPU, memory, storage, pods, persistent volume claims, services, and GPU or MIG resources; scope selectors where approved; and namespace configuration review evidence.
- Owner Primary: Platform Engineering
- Owner Supporting: ML Engineering; FinOps
