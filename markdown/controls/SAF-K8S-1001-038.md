# SAF-K8S-1001-038 - Centralized AI log retention lifecycle enforcement

- Domain: D10 - Observability, Incident Response, and Governance
- Knowledge Area: 10.1 - Logging and Audit
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: baseline

## Statement

Organizations must enforce retention periods aligned with organizational and regulatory requirements for each applicable centralized AI log class.

## Objective

Ensure centralized AI workload logs are retained for required durations so audit, incident response, and record-keeping evidence is not deleted prematurely.

## Rationale

Centralized AI logs lose evidentiary value if retention policies are misconfigured or logs are aged out before required evidence windows expire. Retention lifecycle enforcement preserves availability over the required duration.

## Scope

Applies to centralized logging backends and retained AI workload log classes, including audit logs, training and inference logs, GPU hardware logs, and platform security telemetry retained for operational or regulatory purposes. Covers retention policy configuration and lifecycle enforcement. Excludes append-only immutability, cryptographic integrity verification, and separate durable retrieval testing.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Retention lifecycle enforcement on centralized log stores.
- Implementation Surface: Retention policy configuration per log class, lifecycle enforcement on centralized log stores, and validation that configured retention periods align with organizational and regulatory requirements.
- Owner Primary: Security Operations
- Owner Supporting: Platform Engineering; Infrastructure Engineering
