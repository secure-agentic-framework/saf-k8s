# NIST AI Risk Management Framework 1.0 - GOVERN 4.2

- Framework Code: NIST-AI-RMF
- Requirement Title: Organizational teams document the risks and potential impacts of the AI technology they design, develop, deploy, or use, and communicate about the impacts more broadly.

## Mapping Notes

STRIDE and OCTAVE assessments produce documented risk analysis. ML threat taxonomy mapping documents AI-specific risks. Compliance mapping workbooks communicate risk posture.
