# NIST AI Risk Management Framework 1.0 - MAP 1.1

- Framework Code: NIST-AI-RMF
- Requirement Title: Intended purposes, potentially beneficial uses, context of use, and design goals of the AI system and its expected users are understood and documented. Assumptions and related limitations about these purposes, uses, context of use, and design goals are enumerated and documented.

## Mapping Notes

AI system classification assigns control profiles based on system type, risk tier, and intended use, requiring documentation of system purpose and scope.
