# NIST AI Risk Management Framework 1.0 - GOVERN 1.4

- Framework Code: NIST-AI-RMF
- Requirement Title: The risk management process and its outcomes are established through transparent policies, procedures, and other controls based on organizational risk priorities.

## Mapping Notes

STRIDE and OCTAVE threat modeling establish risk management processes. Automated audit readiness dashboards provide transparency into risk posture and control status.
