# NIST AI Risk Management Framework 1.0 - MANAGE 1.4

- Framework Code: NIST-AI-RMF
- Requirement Title: Negative residual risks (defined as the sum of all unmitigated risks) to both downstream acquirers of AI systems and end users are documented.
