# NIST AI Risk Management Framework 1.0 - MAP 3.1

- Framework Code: NIST-AI-RMF
- Requirement Title: Potential benefits and costs, including potential harms to individuals, communities, organizations, and society, are understood and documented by the team or function performing the mapping.

## Mapping Notes

STRIDE threat modeling systematically identifies threats across spoofing, tampering, repudiation, information disclosure, DoS, and elevation of privilege. OCTAVE assessment includes business impact analysis for identified risks.
