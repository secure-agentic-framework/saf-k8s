# EU AI Act 2024/1689 - Article-6.1.a

- Framework Code: EU-AI-ACT
- Requirement Title: Classification rules for high-risk AI systems

## Mapping Notes

the AI system is intended to be used as a safety component of a product, or the AI system is itself a product, covered by the Union harmonisation legislation listed in Annex I;

SAF-K8S workload labeling and metadata controls can tag AI system components that are safety-critical. Cross-referencing with other harmonisation legislation is an organizational responsibility.
