# EU AI Act 2024/1689 - Article-55.1.b

- Framework Code: EU-AI-ACT
- Requirement Title: Obligations of providers of general-purpose AI models with systemic risk

## Mapping Notes

assess and mitigate possible systemic risks at Union level, including their sources, that may stem from the development, the placing on the market, or the use of general-purpose AI models with systemic risk;

SAF-K8S provides risk management infrastructure through security control catalogs, compliance mappings, and continuous monitoring. The risk management narrative and residual risk documentation are organizational deliverables.
