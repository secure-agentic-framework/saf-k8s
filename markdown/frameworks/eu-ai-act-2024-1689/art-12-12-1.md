# EU AI Act 2024/1689 - Art-12:12(1)

- Framework Code: EU-AI-ACT
- Requirement Title: Record-keeping

## Mapping Notes

High-risk AI systems shall technically allow for the automatic recording of events (logs) over the lifetime of the system.

Kubernetes audit logging, container logging, and AI workload telemetry (SAF-K8S D10 KA 10.1, 10.2) provide the infrastructure for automatic event recording. However, the requirement is that the AI system itself allows for automatic recording of events. This requires application-layer instrumentation to generate AI-specific events (inference decisions, confidence scores, model state changes) beyond what platform logging captures.
