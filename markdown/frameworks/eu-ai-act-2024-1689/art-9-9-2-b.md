# EU AI Act 2024/1689 - Art-9:9(2)(b)

- Framework Code: EU-AI-ACT
- Requirement Title: Risk Management System

## Mapping Notes

The risk management system shall comprise the estimation and evaluation of the risks that may emerge when the high-risk AI system is used in accordance with its intended purpose, and under conditions of reasonably foreseeable misuse.

SAF-K8S threat modeling addresses infrastructure-level risk estimation. Misuse risk evaluation for the AI system's intended purpose is an application-layer concern.
