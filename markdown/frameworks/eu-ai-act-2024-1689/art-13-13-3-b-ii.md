# EU AI Act 2024/1689 - Art-13:13(3)(b)(ii)

- Framework Code: EU-AI-ACT
- Requirement Title: Transparency and Provision of Information to Deployers

## Mapping Notes

The instructions shall describe the level of accuracy, including its metrics, robustness and cybersecurity referred to in Article 15 against which the high-risk AI system has been tested and validated and which can be expected, and any known and foreseeable circumstances that may have an impact on that expected level.

SAF-K8S ML-BOM (SAF-K8S-0604-002) captures accuracy metrics, framework versions, and infrastructure context. The obligation to include these in instructions of use is an organizational concern, but SAF-K8S provides the data source.
