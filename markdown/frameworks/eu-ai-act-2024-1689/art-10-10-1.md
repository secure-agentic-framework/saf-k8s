# EU AI Act 2024/1689 - Art-10:10(1)

- Framework Code: EU-AI-ACT
- Requirement Title: Data and Data Governance

## Mapping Notes

High-risk AI systems which make use of techniques involving the training of AI models with data shall be developed on the basis of training, validation and testing data sets that meet the quality criteria referred to in paragraphs 2 to 5 whenever such data sets are used.

Data quality criteria are application-layer concerns. SAF-K8S provides the infrastructure to enforce data governance: storage access controls, pipeline isolation, data provenance tracking, and data integrity verification (D7, D9 KA 9.6, 9.7).
