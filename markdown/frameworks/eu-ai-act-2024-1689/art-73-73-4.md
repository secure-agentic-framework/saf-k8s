# EU AI Act 2024/1689 - Art-73:73(4)

- Framework Code: EU-AI-ACT
- Requirement Title: Reporting of Serious Incidents

## Mapping Notes

Where the provider considers or has reason to consider that a high-risk AI system that has been placed on the market presents a risk within the meaning of Article 79(1), it shall immediately investigate the cause, in cooperation with the deployer where applicable, and inform the market surveillance authorities of the Member States in which it made available the AI system and, where applicable, the notified body that issued a certificate for the high-risk AI system.

SAF-K8S forensic and logging capabilities (D10) support root cause investigation through audit trails, resource monitoring history, and security event correlation. Cooperation with deployers and notification of authorities are organizational obligations.
