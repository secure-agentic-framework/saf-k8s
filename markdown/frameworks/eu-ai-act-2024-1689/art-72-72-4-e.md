# EU AI Act 2024/1689 - Art-72:72(4)(e)

- Framework Code: EU-AI-ACT
- Requirement Title: Post-market Monitoring by Providers and Post-market Monitoring Plan for High-risk AI Systems

## Mapping Notes

The post-market monitoring plan shall include at least: (e) the identification, investigation and correction of any issues, including serious incidents and malfunctioning, reported by deployers, as well as the implementation of corrective actions, including recall, withdrawal or disabling of the AI system.

SAF-K8S incident response controls (D10 KA 10.5) support incident identification and investigation through logging and alerting. Kubernetes rollback mechanisms (rolling updates, revision history) enable corrective actions including disabling workloads. Full incident management, deployer communication, and recall processes require organizational action.
