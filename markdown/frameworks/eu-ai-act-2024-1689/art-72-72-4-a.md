# EU AI Act 2024/1689 - Art-72:72(4)(a)

- Framework Code: EU-AI-ACT
- Requirement Title: Post-market Monitoring by Providers and Post-market Monitoring Plan for High-risk AI Systems

## Mapping Notes

The post-market monitoring plan shall include at least: (a) the collection of available data regarding the performance and use of the high-risk AI system, taking into account the information provided by deployers through feedback mechanisms referred to in Article 26(5).

SAF-K8S telemetry and logging infrastructure enables collection of performance data (resource utilization, latency, error rates) at the platform level. Deployer feedback mechanisms and usage analytics require application-layer implementation.
