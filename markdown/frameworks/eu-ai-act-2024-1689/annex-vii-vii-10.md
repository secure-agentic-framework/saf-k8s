# EU AI Act 2024/1689 - Annex-VII:VII(10)

- Framework Code: EU-AI-ACT
- Requirement Title: Conformity Based on Assessment of Quality Management System and Assessment of Technical Documentation

## Mapping Notes

The provider shall keep at the disposal of the national competent authorities and the notified body the quality management system assessment certificate and the technical documentation assessment certificate, including any supplements thereto.

SAF-K8S artifact storage and retention controls (D10) support long-term retention infrastructure through persistent storage, backup policies, and access controls. However, the obligation to maintain and make certificates available to authorities is an organizational responsibility.
