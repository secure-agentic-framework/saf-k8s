# EU AI Act 2024/1689 - Art-72:72(1)

- Framework Code: EU-AI-ACT
- Requirement Title: Post-market Monitoring by Providers and Post-market Monitoring Plan for High-risk AI Systems

## Mapping Notes

Providers shall establish and document a post-market monitoring system in a manner that is proportionate to the nature of the AI technologies and the risks of the high-risk AI system.

SAF-K8S observability controls (D10) and audit logging provide the infrastructure telemetry foundation for a post-market monitoring system. However, the full system design, documentation, and proportionality assessment require organizational decisions beyond platform controls.
