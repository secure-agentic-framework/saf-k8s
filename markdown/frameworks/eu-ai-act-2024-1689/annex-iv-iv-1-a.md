# EU AI Act 2024/1689 - Annex-IV:IV(1)(a)

- Framework Code: EU-AI-ACT
- Requirement Title: Technical Documentation Referred to in Article 11(1)

## Mapping Notes

The intended purpose of the AI system, the name and version of the system, and an indication of how the AI system interacts or can be used to interact with hardware or software, including other AI systems, that are not part of the AI system itself.

SAF-K8S tracks workload metadata, container image versions, and service mesh interactions. System-level intended purpose documentation is organizational, but SAF-K8S labels and annotations can capture version and integration topology.
