# EU AI Act 2024/1689 - Art-72:72(4)(c)

- Framework Code: EU-AI-ACT
- Requirement Title: Post-market Monitoring by Providers and Post-market Monitoring Plan for High-risk AI Systems

## Mapping Notes

The post-market monitoring plan shall include at least: (c) the monitoring of the relevance and representativeness of the data used by the AI system, including potential data drift.

SAF-K8S pipeline integrity controls (D9 KA 9.4) and observability tools can detect data volume anomalies and schema changes at the infrastructure level. Statistical data drift detection and representativeness assessment require application-layer ML monitoring tools.
