# EU AI Act 2024/1689 - Art-72:72(4)(b)

- Framework Code: EU-AI-ACT
- Requirement Title: Post-market Monitoring by Providers and Post-market Monitoring Plan for High-risk AI Systems

## Mapping Notes

The post-market monitoring plan shall include at least: (b) the identification of risks associated with the use of the AI system, an assessment of the level of risk, and an assessment of the need for any corrective or preventive actions.

SAF-K8S security monitoring and anomaly detection controls (D10) support identification of infrastructure-level risks (resource exhaustion, security events, performance degradation). Risk assessment methodology and corrective action decisions require organizational processes.
