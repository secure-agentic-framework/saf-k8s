# EU AI Act 2024/1689 - Article-60.8

- Framework Code: EU-AI-ACT
- Requirement Title: Testing of high-risk AI systems in real world conditions outside AI regulatory sandboxes

## Mapping Notes

Providers or prospective providers shall notify the national market surveillance authority in the Member State where the testing in real world conditions is to be conducted of the suspension or termination of the testing in real world conditions and of the final outcomes.

SAF-K8S observability (D10) and experiment tracking infrastructure (D9) capture test execution metrics, performance data, and anomaly records. Analysis and corrective action documentation are organizational deliverables.
