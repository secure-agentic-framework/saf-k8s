# EU AI Act 2024/1689 - Art-12:12(2)(a)

- Framework Code: EU-AI-ACT
- Requirement Title: Record-keeping

## Mapping Notes

Logging capabilities shall enable the recording of events relevant for identifying situations that may result in the high-risk AI system presenting a risk or in a substantial modification.

Kubernetes audit logs, GPU telemetry, and platform monitoring detect configuration drift, resource anomalies, and unauthorized modifications. AI-specific risk events (model behavior changes, output quality shifts) require application-layer logging instrumentation.
