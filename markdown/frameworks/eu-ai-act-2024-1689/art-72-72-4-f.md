# EU AI Act 2024/1689 - Art-72:72(4)(f)

- Framework Code: EU-AI-ACT
- Requirement Title: Post-market Monitoring by Providers and Post-market Monitoring Plan for High-risk AI Systems

## Mapping Notes

The post-market monitoring plan shall include at least: (f) the identification and application of preventive measures, including constraints imposed on the use of the AI system.

SAF-K8S admission control policies and resource quotas can enforce constraints on AI system deployment and usage at the platform level. Identifying which preventive measures are appropriate requires organizational risk assessment.
