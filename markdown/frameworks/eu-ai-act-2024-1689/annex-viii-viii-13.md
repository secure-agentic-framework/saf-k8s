# EU AI Act 2024/1689 - Annex-VIII:VIII(13)

- Framework Code: EU-AI-ACT
- Requirement Title: Information to be Submitted Upon the Registration of High-Risk AI Systems in Accordance with Article 49

## Mapping Notes

Where applicable, information about the high-risk AI system that is a safety component of a product, or is itself a product, and is covered by other Union harmonisation legislation.

SAF-K8S workload labeling and metadata controls can tag AI system components that are safety-critical. Cross-referencing with other harmonisation legislation is an organizational responsibility.
