# NIST SP 800-53 Revision 5 5.2.0 - SC-5(1)

- Framework Code: NIST-800-53R5
- Requirement Title: Restrict Ability to Attack Other Systems

## Mapping Notes

Preventing compromised agents from being used to attack other systems limits lateral movement

5.1 - Egress controls (restricting outbound to prevent attacking other systems); 7.2 - Namespace isolation
