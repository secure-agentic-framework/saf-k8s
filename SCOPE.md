# Scope

This Scope defines the bounds of the SAF-K8S Working Group's work and the corresponding
patent commitments made by Contributors and licensees under the Community Specification
License 1.0 (see [`LICENSE-CSL-1.0.md`](LICENSE-CSL-1.0.md)).

## In Scope

The SAF-K8S Working Group develops and maintains a public security control catalog for
Kubernetes and AI systems, comprising:

- Security controls for Kubernetes and AI workloads, organized by domain and knowledge area;
- Crosswalk mappings between SAF-K8S controls and external security frameworks, standards,
  and benchmarks;
- The supporting taxonomy of domains and knowledge areas; and
- Documentation describing the catalog, its structure, and its intended use.

These materials are published under the `SAF-K8S-*` identifier prefix.

## Out of Scope

- Source code and tooling (including the catalog generation script, `generate_markdown.py`),
  which is licensed separately under the Apache License, Version 2.0
  (see [`LICENSE-APACHE-2.0`](LICENSE-APACHE-2.0)).
- Implementations of the controls described in this catalog.
- Any specification, standard, or other material developed outside this Working Group.

Any changes of Scope are not retroactive.
